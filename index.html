Emotional Intelligence

AI, Deep Learning, NLP, Big Data, consumption analysis, anticipation of desire,...
Digital information processing is today at the heart of any marketing strategy.

All over the world, in every language, thousands of anonymous people
express their enthusiasm or frustration about their experience every day.

WOMSTR is
a platform for
Semantic
Collection|Recognition|Analysis
of opinions

Our mission is to inform your company about the sentiment expressed, and help it stay close to the concerns of your customers, while keeping a watchful eye on the competition.

The WOMSTR tools collect, integrate, classify, and analyze this critical mass of information, allowing you to draw a wealth of more accurate and valuable insights from it.

COLLECTION

Identification of prescribing sites, extraction of reviews thanks to a high-performance and personalised scraping tools that are constantly updated.

PROCESSING & AI

Using our Natural Language Processing (NLP) tools and our constantly improved, deep learning powered thesaurus (the Grid), our software extract information and automatically classify relevant data in multiple levels of details.
Creation and permanent updating of a grid of associations dedicated by sector of activity.
Semantic recognition of information, by a conjugated system of keywords and pairs NOUN/ADJ and NOUN/VERB.

PRESENTATION & ANALYSIS

Generation of standalone and comparative reports (from specific to general), figures and graphs that will allow you to draw your conclusions on the sentiment of your consumers on all aspects at a glance.
